Custom Créu Cursor!

Créu cursors, yes, those that existed back on the old internet, they're back! actually, you can have them on your site! just copy the text inside this box down here, paste it on your page's <head> tag and you're done! 
<script src="https://leninnog.github.io/Creu-Cursor/creu-cursor.js" type="text/javascript"></script>

How does it work?

it's pretty simple, the code linked in that text generates the image objects, and whenever you move the mouse, the code makes the trail images slowly follow it, and also move the Créu image.
